FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a joining fee?

No. There is absolutely no joining fee. You only pay the monthly membership charge and can start training immediately.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All memberships are month-to-month with no lock-in. Cancel anytime by giving 7 days' notice.

Are there annual plan discounts?

Yes! Pay for 10 months and get 2 months free on any plan. Contact us on WhatsApp for annual plan activation.

Can I upgrade my plan?

Absolutely. You can upgrade from Basic to Pro or Elite at any time. The difference is prorated and applied to your next billing cycle.

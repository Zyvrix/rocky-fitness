Meet the Founder

Rakesh Gupta (Rocky)

Founder & Head Trainer, Rocky Fitness Center

Rakesh Gupta (Rocky) founded this center with a simple belief: fitness should be accessible, enjoyable, and transformative for everyone. With over a decade of experience in sports science and personal training, Rocky built this center from the ground up.

His philosophy combines cutting-edge training methodologies with a deeply personal approach — because every member's journey is unique. Today, Rocky Fitness Center stands as a benchmark for premium fitness experiences.

Rocky holds certifications in Strength & Conditioning, Sports Nutrition, and Functional Movement — and continues to train members personally every week.
